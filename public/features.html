Everything your table needs

One toolkit, fully included. No tiers hiding the features that make a session shine.

The map

A living battle map

🗺️

Shared map & tokens

Everyone sees the same board in real time. Pan, zoom, and drag tokens that snap to the grid.

💡

Dynamic lighting

Draw light-blocking walls and drop light sources; players see only what their light reaches.

🌫️

Fog of war

Hide the map and paint to reveal areas as the party explores. Reset it any time.

⊞

Adjustable grid & snapping

Overlay a grid on any map, size it to fit, and snap tokens cleanly into place.

📏

Measure & rotate

Drag to measure distance in feet or squares, and rotate maps to any orientation.

🖼️

Map library & uploads

Upload your own maps or paste a URL, and save a shared library to reuse across sessions.

Dice & combat

Roll, track, and run the fight

🎲

True 3D physics dice

Dice tumble and settle on the real result, with collectible skins. Or use the lighter 2D animation.

🎯

Advantage & secret rolls

Roll with advantage/disadvantage in a click, or make secret rolls only you and the DM see.

⚡

One-click macros

Save frequent rolls — “Longsword: 1d20+5” — as buttons. Type full dice notation in chat too.

📜

Initiative tracker

Roll initiative for everyone at once, step through turns, and highlight the active token on the map.

🔺

Spell templates

Drop circles and cones sized in feet to show exactly who's in the blast.

⏱️

Turn timer

Keep turns moving with a shared countdown that everyone can see.

Characters & content

Built for D&D 5e

📋

Character sheets

Full 5e sheets — abilities, AC, HP, skills, spells, equipment — saved to your table.

🐲

Bestiary

Keep monster & NPC stat blocks handy and drag creatures straight onto the map as tokens.

📖

Quick reference

Look up spells and magic items without leaving your game.

✨

AI assistant

Generate rules-legal characters and monsters, get rulings, and brainstorm encounters.

📓

Journal & notes

Private GM notes and lore, with a toggle to share entries with players.

💰

Party loot

Track the hoard, item values, who's carrying what, and the party gold.

Atmosphere & table

Set the mood

🎙️

Voice & video

Talk and share video right in the browser — no extra app to launch.

🔊

Soundboard

Ambient loops and sound effects you can play for the whole table.

🌦️

Weather & effects

Overlay rain, snow, or mist, and flash spell effects across everyone's screen.

💡

Philips Hue sync

Optional helper flashes your real smart lights to match spells and scenes.

🖼️

Handouts

Reveal an image — a letter, a map, a portrait — full-screen to the party.

👥

Accounts & roles

Player, Game Master, and admin roles with table invites and access control.
