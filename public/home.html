The battle map

Dynamic lighting, fog & line-of-sight

Drop in a map, paint to reveal as the party explores, and draw light-blocking walls so players only see what their torches reach. Place light sources, rotate maps, snap tokens to an adjustable grid, and measure distance with a drag.

Roll in style

True 3D physics dice

Dice tumble across the screen and settle on the real result — with a half-dozen collectible skins (galaxy, crimson, emerald and more). Advantage, disadvantage, secret rolls, and one-click macros are all built in. Prefer it lighter? Switch to the classic 2D animation.

Your AI co-pilot

Generate characters, monsters & rulings

Describe a character or creature and the built-in assistant builds a complete, rules-legal 5e stat block in seconds. Stuck on a rule mid-session? Ask it. Need a quick encounter idea? It's right there in the sidebar.

Set the scene

Voice, video, sound & smart lights

Talk and share video right in the browser. Trigger ambient loops and sound effects from the soundboard, flash spell effects on screen — and even sync your real Philips Hue lights to the action with the optional helper.
