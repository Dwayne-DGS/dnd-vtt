🎲 D&D Hue Helper

Keep this program running during your game. It listens for spell effects and flashes your Hue lights.

1. Connect to your game

Not connected

2. Pair your Hue Bridge

Not paired

Click "Find it" to auto-detect the bridge, press the round button on top of the bridge, then click Pair.

3. Choose lights

Check the lights you want effects on. None checked = all lights.

"Normal" is what the lights return to after an effect. Set your usual ambiance, then click this.

4. Test effects

Fire an effect on your lights right now (no game needed).
